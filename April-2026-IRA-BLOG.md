## **What is Alkaline Water? (Complete Guide)**

**Alkaline water** refers to water with a **higher pH level (typically
between 8 and 9.5)** compared to regular drinking water (neutral pH of
7). This elevated pH level is believed to help neutralize acidity in the
body.

### **Key Features of Alkaline Water:**

- Higher pH level than regular water

- Contains essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium

- Often enriched with electrolytes

- Available as natural or ionized water

## **Types of Alkaline Water Available in India**

### **1. Natural Alkaline Water**

- Sourced from mountains or natural springs

- Naturally enriched with minerals

- Maintains stable pH levels

### **2. Ionized Alkaline Water**

- Processed using RO and ionization technology

- Artificially increases pH

- May lack natural mineral balance

## **Benefits of Alkaline Water**

The growing popularity of [**alkaline water
benefits**](https://www.healthline.com/health/food-nutrition/alkaline-water-benefits-risks)
comes from its association with:

### **1. Enhanced Hydration**

Electrolytes and minerals may support better hydration compared to
regular water.

### **2. Acid Neutralization**

Helps balance acidity levels in the body (as per consumer trends).

### **3. Improved Digestion**

Minerals like bicarbonates can aid digestion and reduce acidity
discomfort.

### **4. Energy & Recovery**

Popular among fitness enthusiasts for hydration and post-workout
recovery.

### **5. Detox Support**

Often linked with improved metabolic function and toxin elimination.

👉 While scientific evidence is still evolving, alkaline water remains a
preferred lifestyle choice for many.
